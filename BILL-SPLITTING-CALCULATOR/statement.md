#Bill Spplitting Calculator

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Managing shared expenses among friends, roomamtes, or small groups can often lead to confusion, miscalculations, or disputes. People need a simple reliable way to fairly divide bills without manual calculations or misunderstandings.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT

This project focuses on providing a straightforward tool to split bills accurately among multiple participants. It calculates each person's share based on contributions and total expenses. It can be extended in the future with a GUI, mobile app version, or support for recurring bills and expense tracking.

TARGET USERS

- College students or roommates sharing rent, utilities, and groceries.
- Friends or family splitting group meals, trips or events.
- Small teams or colleagues managing shared office expenses.

HIGH LEVEL FEATURES

- Input participant names and their contribution.
- Enter total bill amount
- Automatically calculate the amount owed or to be received by each participant.
- Display a clear summary of the bill split for transparency.
